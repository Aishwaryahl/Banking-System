Our Mission Statement

We have a two-fold objective: first, to be the preferred provider of banking services for target retail and wholesale customer segments. The second objective is to achieve healthy growth in profitability, consistent with the bank's risk appetite.

Vision Mission
To emerge as a Preferred Bank by pursuing global benchmarks in profitability, operational efficiency, asset quality, risk management and expanding the global reach. To provide quality banking services with good customer care, create value for all stakeholders and continue as a responsive corporate social citizen.
